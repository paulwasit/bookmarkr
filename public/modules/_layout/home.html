About this blog

Tremendous amounts of data are generated each day, from businesses and individuals alike. Harnessing its value is increasingly vital to innovation and competitive edge: generate actionnable information from evidence-based insights.

Data Science helps leveraging data to answer an organization-specific problem, from evaluating a new product using sales figures and call center logs to detecting instrument failure using various sensors reading. A succesful Data Science project starts by identifying the opportunities, defining the priorities and building a team of both data scientists and domain experts.

Exploratory analysis uncovers usable signals and meaningful insights. Understanding the internal structure of data and interacting with domain experts leads to a better understanding of the studied field. This is the key to efficienly build the optimal mathematical model to solve the problem.

The last, but certainly not the least, step of a succesful Data Science projet is to share the algorithms outputs through the organization. Building a dashboard with custom visualizations helps people to take decisions faster, more efficiently, while removing the often tedious task of rebuilding KPIs manually.

In this blog, I aim to share insights about all these aspects: translating a question into a data science problem, algorithms, machine learning, data science languages (r & python) and front end language (JavaScript/AngularJS/D3).
